What is Twitch Buds?

Created by Gwardo420, Twitch Buds helps streamers worldwide collaborate and grow their communities.

I started streaming in 2017 on Twitch full time. Everybody was promoting themselves and asking for F4F and lurks. While this will help get your numbers really high, it will not help your channel be successful in the long run. Create great content and people will be back to see it. Collab with other streamers to help get your name out there in a professional organic way and you will see a big improvement on your channel.

This is where Twitch Buds thrives in helping streamers reach each other to engage in collaborations.

Please note: If you join Twitch Buds without engaging with the community, you won't see much improvement on your channel. Twitch Buds IS NOT a view bot. Put in the effort to engage with the community and you will see an improvement!

Commands:

Example: t!twitchsearch Shroud

t!bal - Show's your BudCoin's balance!

Example: t!bal

t!daily - Give's you 1,000 daily Discord Points.

Example: t!cash

t!coinflip - Flip's a coin for a bet. Do you have the guts?

Example: t!coinflip heads 100

t!stats - Show's your Twitch Buds server stats!

Exmaple: t!stats

t!twitch - Explain's how to setup your Twitch Account to appear in the LIVE-NOW channel.

Example: t!twitch

Other Information:

Live Now Section:

Link your Twitch to your Discord & Enable Streamer Mode in your User Settings Twitch Buds Bot will automatically pick show you in the LIVE NOW section and tweet out your twitch link.

Please note that Tweets have a 5 minute cool down, that means if someone goes live before you do it will take 5 mins to send another tweet out.

This is due to the Twitter API and will be accessed in the future to allow maximum capicity. Thank you for understanding.
