What is Twitch Buds?

Created by Gwardo420, Twitch Buds helps streamers worldwide collaborate and grow their communities.

I created Twitch Buds to help streamers that post their LIVE link on Twitter. Right now it just focuses on Twitch but soon Youtube will also be implemented into the App.

Discord members get tweeted when they go live if they are following the Twitch Buds twitter account.

Please note: Just generally engaging in audience will increase follow/viewership retention!

Discord Information:

Commands:

t!twitchsearch - Search's a Twitch Channel name.

Example: t!twitchsearch Shroud

t!bal - Show's your BudCoin's balance!

Example: t!bal

t!daily - Give's you 1,000 daily Discord Points.

Example: t!daily

t!coinflip - Flip's a coin for a bet. Do you have the guts?

Example: t!coinflip heads 100

t!stats - Show's your Twitch Buds server stats!

Exmaple: t!stats

t!twitch - Explain's how to setup your Twitch Account to appear in the LIVE-NOW channel.

Example: t!twitch

BudCoins Information:

This is our Community currency, you can use these to Redeem things in the Discord Store.

These can be earned by Chatting in Discord. One chat message gives you 1 BudCoin. You can also play the Points-Game in the Twitch Buds Discord - you can then exchange these points for BudCoins!
