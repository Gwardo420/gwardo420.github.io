How to Earn:

You can earn BudCoins just chatting in the Discord! You get 1 BudCoin per chat message sent!

We have a game in the Discord called Points-Game that is made by Gwardo420. You can earn points by reacting to messages, then you can exchange these points for BudCoins.

After you collect some BudCoins you can head on over to the Discord store to redeem thing such as a Twitter shoutout that will Tweet your Twitch Link to over 11k followers on Twitter.
